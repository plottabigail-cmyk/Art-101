Once upon a time, there was a vast green feild with many creatures. It was taken over by gross humans who built over it with stones and wood they gained by cutting down nearby trees. The creatures of the garden weren't very happy about that so they came together to overthrow the humans. Now, the Garden is repopulated with many faries and beasts who dance around the abandoned statues and castles, living in the beautiful greenery in peace once again.
